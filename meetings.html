Meetings & Events

Versatile Event Spaces

Located opposite Exeter Central station, The Rougemont Hotel by Sunday offers two elegant meeting rooms, the Cavendish suite and Devonshire suite, with modern facilities, perfect for conferences, seminars, and events, accommodating up to 250 guests in theatre style.

Our Venues

Dedicated Meeting Rooms


Devonshire suite

Ideal for large conferences with a capacity of 250 theatre style, 220 Banquet and 160 Cabaret (8 per table). The room also has a stage for presentations and offer a private reception(bar) area upon entrance.

Cavendish Suite

This is our second largest room, capacity is 120 Theatre, 100 banquet and 64 Cabaret style. The room also offer a private bar area.
