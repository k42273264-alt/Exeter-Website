Getting Here

Arrive Easily

Nearest station: Exeter Central - 0.1 miles

Local buses connect to Exeter city centre, stopping near Queen Street.

From M5:

Exit at Junction 30 (Exeter).

Follow A379 towards Exeter city centre.

Turn onto Queen Street; the hotel is opposite Exeter Central station.

5 miles to Exeter International Airport.

Pet-Friendly

Welcome Four-Legged Guests

Our dog-friendly rooms come with treats, bowls, and access to Exeter’s countryside walks. Dogs must be on leads in public areas and supervised (max two dogs under 15kg, £20/night per pet, service dogs free).
