Pet Policy

Bring Your Pet to Exeter

Welcome to The Rougemont Hotel by Sunday

We are delighted to welcome you and your four-legged companions to our hotel, located on Queen Street opposite Exeter Central station, with easy access to Northernhay Gardens for scenic walks. Please review the details below for a comfortable stay with your pets.

Allowed Dogs

  • Dogs are allowed in all room types (Standard Double, Standard Twin, Superior Double, Superior Family, and Suites).
  • A maximum of two dogs per room; no weight restriction specified unless prior approval is obtained from hotel management.
  • A £20 per night fee applies per dog.
  • Service dogs are free of charge.
  • Dogs are welcome in designated lounge areas but not in the main restaurant (except service dogs). They must be on leads at all times.
  • Dogs cannot be left alone in rooms. Housekeeping will only clean rooms when dogs are not present.

Guest Responsibilities, Damages, and Cleaning

  • Dogs must be supervised and on leads at all times within the hotel premises.
  • Owners are responsible for ensuring dogs do not disturb other guests with excessive barking.
  • Owners must clean up after dogs and dispose of waste properly (disposal bags provided).
  • Guests are liable for any damage caused by dogs.
  • A cleaning fee may apply for extra cleaning or damage after check-out, amount to be determined by management.

Restrictions

  • Only dogs are allowed as pets.
  • Assistance dogs are exempt from charges and restrictions.

Amenities

  • Disposal bags provided.
  • Information on nearby Northernhay Gardens walks.

By bringing a dog, guests agree to these terms. Thank you for helping us maintain a pleasant environment for all guests.
