Festive Celebrations

A Magical Christmas in Exeter

Celebrate the festive season at The Rougemont Hotel by Sunday, opposite Exeter Central station and near Exeter Cathedral. Our Drakes Restaurant & Bar, Cathedral Suite, and Devon Suite offer the perfect setting for party nights, private gatherings, and festive lunches. Enjoy seasonal menus, elegant surroundings, and a warm atmosphere with 98 rooms.
