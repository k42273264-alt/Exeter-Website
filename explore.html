Exeter Adventures

Experience Exeter’s Charm

From the majestic Exeter Cathedral to the historic Underground Passages, Exeter offers a rich blend of history, culture, and scenic beauty. Stay at The Rougemont Hotel by Sunday, located opposite Exeter Central station, and explore the city’s iconic landmarks and nearby trails, perfect for a memorable getaway with or without your four-legged friends.

Local Attractions

Discover Exeter

Exeter Cathedral

A stunning Gothic cathedral with a breathtaking vaulted ceiling, just a 5-minute walk from the hotel.

Underground Passages

Explore medieval tunnels beneath the city, offering a unique historical experience, located 0.3 miles away.

Royal Albert Memorial Museum

An award-winning museum showcasing art, history, and culture, just a 7-minute walk from the hotel.
