Getting Here

Travel to The Rougemont Hotel

Nearest station: Exeter Central – 0.1 miles / 2-minute walk.

Local buses connect to Exeter city centre, stopping near Queen Street.

From M5:

Exit at Junction 30 (Exeter).

Follow A379 towards Exeter city centre.

Turn onto Queen Street; the hotel is opposite Exeter Central station.

Nearest airport: Exeter International Airport (EXT) – 5 miles / 15-minute drive.
